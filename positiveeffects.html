Jainism for Youth: Building a Positive Future

Fostering Resilience and Positive Change in Society

Dayabhav

Dayabhav teaches individuals to show kindness and
empathy towards all living beings.
It encourages a lifestyle rooted in care, promoting
non-violence and understanding in every action.

Sanyam

Sanyam helps in cultivating inner strength by controlling
one's desires and impulses. Through self-restraint,
individuals can focus on their goals and lead a
balanced, thoughtful life.

Paryavaran Sanrakshan

Jainism emphasizes the importance of protecting the
environment as part of a moral duty. This principle
encourages youth to respect nature and live sustainably,
ensuring the well-being of all forms of life.

Shanti

Shanti focuses on achieving inner peace and spreading
harmony in one's surroundings. By adopting
non-violence and peaceful thoughts, individuals
create a positive and stress-free life for
themselves and others.

Anekantvad

Anekantvad encourages understanding that truth has many
sides and fosters respect for diverse viewpoints.
This principle helps develop critical thinking,
promoting intellectual flexibility and open-mindedness

Dharma

Dharma is the ethical code that guides individuals
to make morally sound decisions. It is the path of
righteousness, helping people lead lives of honesty,
integrity, and responsibility.

Aparigraha

Aparigraha teaches the importance of non-attachment to
material possessions, emphasizing a simple and content
life. By letting go of excess, individuals can focus on
spiritual growth and reduce unnecessary desires.

Adhyatmikta

Adhyatmikta encourages individuals to engage in
introspection, meditation, and self-realization.
It is the journey towards spiritual enlightenment,
allowing a deeper connection with oneself and the
universe.
